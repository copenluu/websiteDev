Strays Meet Holland Awareness

These are all different animals that have
been saved by Strays Meet Holland, each
with their own story. It is important to the
charity to make sure that every animal they
can is rescued and provided a safe
environment where they can recover until
they are eventually ready to find their forever
homes!

This is only a small sample of the many, many,
animals that have been saved by Strays Meet Holland,
and there is so much work they have done that is not
even documented on their website, but instead on their
Facebook page which can be found just by searching
“Strays Meet Holland”, along with their actual site.
