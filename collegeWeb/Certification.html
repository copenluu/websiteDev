ANBI CERTIFICATION

Strays Meet Holland has achieved ANBI status from the Dutch government.

This means that the charity has certain tax benefits,
one of which can be be seen below, taken directly
from the site

"Since April 2014 we have the ANBI status.
This means that all donations are tax deductible.
You are most welcome as a donor to our foundation,
whether this is structural or incidental. We are happy with
any donation with which we can help our stray animals.
Our bank details can be found in the contact details"
