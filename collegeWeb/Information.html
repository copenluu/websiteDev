Strays Meet Holland Awareness

Strays Meet Holland is an organisation run by volunteers that rescue and care for stray animals in the Netherlands, southern Spain, and Romania. They primarily take in stray cats in the Netherlands at their own shelter with the hopes of rehoming them once they are ready and providing a better life for them after all they have experienced.

Abroad, Strays Meet Holland supports 2 permanent foreign shelters by sending things such financial support and relief, supplies like medicine, food, and other accommodations like beds for animals and leads/collars.

Throughout this site I aim to raise awareness for them and to showcase the work that they do in 3 different countries.
